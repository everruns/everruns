# Everruns Brand Identity

## Brand Essence

**Name:** Everruns — AI agents that **ever run**. Continuous, uninterrupted, eternal execution.

**Tagline:** "Durable AI. Unstoppable agents."

**Positioning:** The durability layer for AI agents. While others focus on making agents smarter, we make them **unkillable**.

---

## Logo

Three interlocking rings (Borromean rings pattern) — an unbreakable chain representing **Durability × Scalability × Reliability**.

Each ring has a gradient from **navy on the outside to gold towards the center** — symbolizing how the three qualities converge into one unified platform.

### Files
- `logo.svg` — Color version with gradients
- `logo-mono.svg` — Black & white version

### Usage
- **Color version**: Marketing, website, social media
- **Mono version**: Documentation, favicons, dark backgrounds
