## Keywords for Logo Generators

**Style**: minimal, geometric, clean, modern, tech, precise, flat
**Mood**: reliable, persistent, infinite, continuous, unbreakable

---

## Usage Tips

1. Generate multiple variations
2. Pick 3-5 favorites
3. Recreate best ones as clean SVG in Figma/Illustrator
4. Test at multiple sizes (16px to 512px)
5. Verify it works in both light and dark modes
